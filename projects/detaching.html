Detaching, 2023
A sample pack featuring 145 sounds and the result of a collaboration between Popular Music students at the University of Gloucestershire, investigating the role of music for social change. Inspired by the blog post 'Sample Packing as Social Practice' by Max Alper, the students produced a suite of sounds with the intention of making them publicly available.

The pack features environmental recordings of several places selected by the students, and include a variety of textures, pops, and klangs. Samples are organised alphabetically, and named after the action taking place in the recording.
