Detaching, 2023
This sample pack is the result of a collaboration between Popular Music students at the University of Gloucestershire, investigating the role of music for social change. Inspired by the blog post 'Sample Packing as Social Practice' by Max Alper, the students prodcued a suite of sounds with the intention of making them publicly available.

The sample pack is a small experiment within a larger project that imagines future music practices, making statements about existing models within the music industries in relation to sustainability frameworks.
