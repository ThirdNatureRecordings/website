Third Nature
"third nature", that is, what manages to live despite capitalism (Anna Tsing, 2017)
Third Nature produces forward thinking music projects, and is built on the principle that cooperation creates healthier music communities.

Guided by mutualist values and perspectives on eco-feminism, Third Nature fosters collaboration, knowledge sharing, and ecological interdependence among artists, allowing for flexible participation and decentralised decision-making.

Embracing a cooperative-community model, Third Nature combines the stability of a core co-op structure with the flexibility of an inclusive, open-ended community.
